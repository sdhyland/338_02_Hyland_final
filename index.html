The adventure waits.

Beautiful Montana De Oro has miles of adventure waiting for yourself, friends, and family to explore. There are countless trails and camp sites that are here for you to enjoy. Montana De Oros Valencia Peak is the tallest peak in the whole park. from the top of 1347 foot high peak you are able to have a 360 view all the way Morro Rock to the back trails of the park and Diablo Canyon and back around. Another popular actration on the park grounds is Spooners Cove. The cove has a beachy stretch of shore to walk along side the water and a popular spot for locals to sit along the beach and watch the colorful breath taking sunsets.

Days:Seven days a weekHours:6 a.m.- 10 p.m.

IMPORTANT: Keep in mind that if you plan on bringing a dog to the park they must stay on a leash that is no longer than six feet in length and accompanied by an adult. Dogs are only permitted inside the camp grounds, on a leash on the paved roads, and Spooners beach. Dogs are not permitted to be left alone on the camp grounds or anywhere else in the park, you must be with them at all times.
